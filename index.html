"To be in CSHS is to discover, learn, and challenge."

Discover

Computer Science is all about breaking the barriers and pushing the frontier of discovery. CSHS encourages its members to challenge themselves and seek solutions to never solved before problems.

Learn

Learning is the foundation of computer science, whether it be learning from others, learning through programming, or even a machine learning from a programmer! CSHS helps push the boundaries of what CS can teach by extending and providing opportunities to learn even the most complex topics in CS.

Challenge

Finally, to code is to challenge. Computer Science is filled with challenges every day. It is an industry that requires extensive problem solving skills, helping shape collaborative team members and life-long learners. In CSHS, challenges are no stranger to us, but we face them, head on.

The Travis High School Computer Science Honor Society promotes and cultivates dedicated computer-science students by being members of a collaborative CS community through participation in opportunities to extend their learning.

What CSHS Means to Me...

  • The opportunity to earn scholarships.
  • The opportunity to join a community of CS-oriented students.
  • The opportunity to collaborate and create life-changing projects.
  • The opportunity to partake in various national/international competitions.
  • The opportunity to form life-long friendships.
  • The opportunity to hear from others in the industry.
  • The opportunity to challenge myself, personally and academically.
